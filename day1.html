29 March 2021

This is what we did at EA.

We 1st learnt to create our own website. Then we learnt to use Autodesk Fusion 360 to do some 3D modelling and finally 3D printed a little key chain with our names on it.
The following are some random notes I took.
Methods to print things on shirts:

  • Screen printing
  • Heat transfer printing
  • Stencilling
  • Embroidery
  • Dye sublimation
  • Direct to garment

    • Colours in printing:
  • CMYK-Cyan Magenta Yellow Black
  • RGB-Red Green Blue
